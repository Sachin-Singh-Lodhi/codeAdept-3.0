About

With the changes taking place in the campus recruitments, it's high time to get your skills sharpened!


CodeAdept is a core technical event solely conducted by the Department of Information Technology of University Institute of Technology, RGPV which has now become a legacy. The incentives of the competition is to provide a platform for ameriolating your coding skills and to get an idea of the changing recruitment scenario.


CodeAdept and CodeAdept 2.0 was a great success under the guidance of Dr. Ashmita Moghe, Dr. Anjana Pandey, Dr. Roopam Gupta and Dr. Sachin Goyal and under the mentorship of Mr. Vikas Rohit and Mr. Amit Namdev.


The major branches that were involved in the competition were IT, CS, EC and MCA who not only participated with great enthusiasm but also showcased their programming skills.


So here we are back again with CODEADEPT 3.0 to calm your frenzied minds with the same exuberance as last years. Get yourself registered and unleash the geek in you.


Features:

● Get an idea of the changing recruitment scenario.

● Sharpen your coding skills.

● Grab exciting prizes and qualification certificates.

Pre-requisites:

Basic knowledge of aptitude, logical reasoning, English proficiency and coding languages like C, C++, Java and Python.


About the competition:

ROUND 1: This round will contain questions based on quants, logical reasoning, English proficiency. And some basic coding questions. There will be 30 questions that must be solved within 30 mins.


ROUND 2: The participants who qualifies 1st round will be eligible for this round. There will be 40 questions based on debugging and coding output that must be solved within 45 mins.


ROUND 3: The 2nd round qualifiers will then implement code based on real life problems given, in preferred coding language (C, C++, Java, Python). There will be 3 questions that must be solved within 1.5 hrs.


Round 1 and Round 2 will be conducted on first day (27th Sept'19) and the final round on the second day (28th Sept’19).


The participants will be selected at each round and will be promoted to the next rounds. Participants who will clear first round will be awarded participation certificates


The top three winners of the competition will be awarded with certificates and a cash prize of worth INR 6000.
